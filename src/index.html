Green Restaurant, our vision

After a gastronomic "Tour de France" with top starred chefs, we fell in love with Saint-Emilion and decided to put our bags there. It is therefore in this 16th century stone house that we are pleased to welcome you in a warm atmosphere and, in summer, in our shaded patio. We offer modern, vegetal cuisine made with local and seasonal products. We are committed to investing ourselves daily in a global eco-responsible approach.

This is why, in the search for our partners, we favor women and men committed to respecting the environment and participate in promoting their work and their products.

We also select our wines on these criteria. We invite you to discover the wines of our region but also from elsewhere: wines from organic farming, biodynamic ...
